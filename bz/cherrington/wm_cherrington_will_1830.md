# Last will and testament of William Taylor Cherrington (1830)

I, W.T. Cherrington of the settlement of Belize in Honduras aforesay and hereby make this my last will and testament, hereby revoking all former wills by me made.

After my just and lawful debts are paid, I will and bequeath upon Phoebe Flowers and my eight children begotten of her body, Eleanor, William, Sarah Ann, Elizabeth, Emma, Henry Usher, John Stanley, and James Cherrington, the whole of my property whatsoever, to share and share alike, and thereby nominate and constitute and appoint Phoebe Flowers aforesaid my sole executer.

Written in my hand this thirtieth day of September in the year of our Lord, one thousand eight hundred and thirty, signed at Belize, Honduras, on the day and date above written.

W.T. Cherrington (signature)
