# Research questions
*Last updated: 06.12.2025

## Questions
1. How are contemporary Belizeans descended from colonial era Belizeans from the late 1700s and the early 1800s?
2. How is the DNA of colonial era Belizeans represented in the DNA of contemporary Belizeans?
3. How do we model the growth of colonial era Belizean families (e.g., the Flowers family) through to present day?
4. What are the genetic admixtures of contemporary Belizeans and how do these compare with colonial era Belizeans?
